A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, full name Dr Avul Pakija Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, Was an Indian scientist, politician, writer and teacher. He took birth on 15 October 1931 at Rameswaram in Madras Presidency. This place is now in Ramanathapuram District, Tamil Nadu. His father was a boat owner and imam (An Islamic leader) of a local mosque. He was the youngest of four brothers and a sister. Abdul Kalam was an average student in school but his learning attitude was exceptional. He used to spend hours studying. His favourite subject was mathematics. 1955 Abdul Kalam went to the Madras Institute of Technology to study Aerospace Engineering.
